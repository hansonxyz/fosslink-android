# Privacy Policy

**FossLink Android Companion App**

*Last updated: March 17, 2026*

## Overview

FossLink is a local-network SMS/MMS companion app. It connects your Android phone to your computer over your local Wi-Fi network to send and receive text messages from your desktop. FossLink does not use cloud servers, does not require an account, and does not transmit any data over the internet.

## Data Collection

FossLink does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. Specifically:

- **No analytics or telemetry** — The app does not contain any analytics SDKs or tracking code.
- **No advertising** — The app contains no ads or ad-related SDKs.
- **No cloud services** — All communication happens directly between your phone and your computer over your local network.
- **No account required** — The app does not require registration or sign-in.

## Data Accessed on Your Device

To function, FossLink requests access to the following on-device data. This data is only transmitted to your paired desktop computer over your local Wi-Fi network:

- **SMS/MMS messages** — To display and send text messages from your desktop.
- **Contacts** — To show contact names alongside conversations.
- **Phone state** — To detect incoming calls.
- **Notifications** — To show connection and pairing status notifications.
- **Files and media** — To browse your phone's gallery and files from your desktop (optional).

None of this data leaves your local network. It is transmitted directly from your phone to your paired desktop over an encrypted connection.

## Device Pairing

FossLink uses a local pairing process to establish a trusted connection between your phone and desktop. Pairing information is stored locally on both devices. No pairing data is sent to any external server.

## Third-Party Services

FossLink does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or SDKs that collect user data.

## Children's Privacy

FossLink is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.
